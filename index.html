    Guided Similarity Separation for Image Retrieval
    Chundi Liu, Guangwei Yu, Cheng Chang, Himanshu Rai, Junwei Ma,
    Satya Krishna Gorti, Maksims Volkovs
    Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), 2019 (Oral 2.5%)
